Curiosity Follower, Programmer.

I am Ejay Canaria a software engineer based in the Philippines. I'm not sure what a good introduction is. Maybe let's just discuss why I'm a curiousity follower and what type of programmer I am.

Curiosity Follower

I've been a fan of self learning after I graduated in college. When I saw the real world, I realized that there are tons of things I need to learn, especially in my field of study - software engineering. I study things that I find interesting. I read books (which I usually don't do back in school), watch tutorials and get my hands dirty in coding.

I followed my curiousity ever since. As the old adage said,

Life is a continuous process of learning.

So, don't stop.

Programmer

I code for human - from user experience and under the hood. I believe in KISS (Keep It Simple, Stupid) and YAGNI (You Ain't Gonna Need It). Beauty is just simplicity. I love to write readable codes. Codes that do not need comments. Codes that explain itself.

Good code is like good joke - it needs no explanation.

About
